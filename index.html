Learning Tools &

Three interactive modules developed for Optimize Financial Group's advisor training program. Each demonstrates a different approach to translating regulatory and procedural content into navigable, assessment-ready experiences.

HTML / CSS / JS Instructional Design Financial Services
01
Assessment · Geography

Canada Sales Tax Interactive Map

An interactive SVG map of Canada built from real geographic data. Advisors identify which provinces operate under a non-harmonized sales tax model (separate GST and PST) by clicking directly on the map — correct selections are confirmed in teal, incorrect ones in amber, with a running score tracker.

SVG Tax Compliance Interactive Assessment
02
Simulator · Privacy Compliance

Redaction Standard Simulator

A side-by-side simulator that demonstrates Optimize's PII redaction standard for client statement submissions. Advisors toggle redaction bars on and off to see exactly which fields must be blacked out before uploading documents — and which fields the transition team still needs visible.

Privacy / PII Compliance Training Simulator
03
Knowledge Check · Privacy Compliance

Redaction Standard Knowledge Check

The assessment counterpart to the simulator. Advisors are presented with a client statement and must independently identify and redact all PII fields. On submission, the module evaluates whether they redacted the right fields — flagging both missed PII and over-redaction of data the transition team requires.
