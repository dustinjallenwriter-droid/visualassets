Three interactive modules developed for Optimize Financial Group's advisor training program. Each translates a regulatory or procedural concept into a navigable, assessment-ready experience.

01
Assessment · Geography

Canada Sales Tax Interactive Map

An interactive SVG map of Canada built from real geographic data. Advisors identify which provinces operate under a non-harmonized sales tax model by clicking directly on the map — correct selections are confirmed, incorrect ones flagged, with a running score tracker.

SVG Tax Compliance Interactive Assessment
02
Simulator · Privacy Compliance

Redaction Standard Simulator

A side-by-side simulator demonstrating Optimize's PII redaction standard for client statement submissions. Advisors toggle redaction bars to see exactly which fields must be blacked out before uploading — and which fields the transition team still needs visible.

Privacy / PII Compliance Training Simulator
03
Knowledge Check · Privacy Compliance

Redaction Standard Knowledge Check

The assessment counterpart to the simulator. Advisors independently identify and redact all PII fields on a client statement. On submission, the module evaluates their work — flagging both missed PII and over-redaction of data the transition team requires.
